Our program needs on average ~800.000 iterations to calculate pi in the given range.

float: 
- 3.141592
- sizeof(float) == 4

double:
- 3.141592653589793
- sizeof(double) == 8

long double:
- 3.141592653589793238
- sizeof(long double) == 16

You need 67 bits of mantissa to encode 20 decimal digits of pi.
